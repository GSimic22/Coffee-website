Nespresso Vertuo vs. Original

What is the difference?


THE CLASSIC ESPRESSO EXPERIENCE THE ORIGINAL EXPERIENCE Original was inspired by Italian roasting traditions. Whether you prefer full-bodied or decaffeinated, black coffee or adding milk to make cappuccinos or lattes, Original is the ideal machine for espresso lovers looking for an easy way to make the perfect coffee every morning with just the touch of a button.

SOURCE OF ENDLESS POSSIBILITIES THE VERTUO EXPERIENCE Do you love big cups of coffee on Monday mornings? Enjoy an espresso, cappuccino or latte in the afternoon? How about a large carafe of coffee when one is not enough? With its endless possibilities, Vertuo adapts to all your desires at the push of a button. It's the perfect machine for coffee lovers who want Nespresso quality in a wide variety of styles and sizes.
